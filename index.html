About NTT Groups Healthcare

Leading the transformation of healthcare management with innovative technology solutions designed for labor camps, remote clinics, and medical facilities across Saudi Arabia and beyond.

Our Mission

To revolutionize healthcare delivery in remote and labor camp settings by providing comprehensive, AI-powered management systems that ensure quality medical care is accessible to everyone, everywhere.

We believe that distance should never be a barrier to healthcare excellence, and our PAMS platform embodies this commitment through cutting-edge technology and user-centric design.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in specialized healthcare management systems, setting new standards for medical service delivery in challenging environments.

We envision a future where every worker, regardless of location, has access to world-class healthcare management and emergency response systems.
